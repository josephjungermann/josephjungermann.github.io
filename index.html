About Me

Get to know me

My name is Joseph Jungermann, and I am a graduate student at the Craig Newmark School of Journalism. I have over four years of writing and editing experience starting as a sports writer and editor for Turner Broadcasting's Bleacher Report. I am currently working as an investigative reporter specializing in data analysis. I cover urban government and politics, criminal justice and housing, and my recent work can be found at the Juvenile Justice Information Exchange and Kansas City, Missouri, publication, The Beacon.
